# Card Atmosphere Visual System

## Purpose

These images are reusable atmosphere assets for conversation cards such as weather and scent. They should feel like quiet lifestyle/editorial photography, not AI posters, decorative fantasy art, or one repeated setup with different props.

## Format

- Square image, 900 x 900 source.
- No text, logo, watermark, UI, hands, faces, or obvious brand objects.
- One clear subject or motif per image.
- Composition must remain readable when cropped to a small card thumbnail.

## Style

- Realistic editorial still life, environmental detail, or lifestyle-adjacent scene crop.
- Natural imperfect materials: paper, glass, ceramic, linen, stone, wood, metal, rain, steam, petals.
- Controlled negative space, restrained props, no dense scenes.
- Soft natural light with one color accent. Avoid neon, heavy glow, fantasy mist, cinematic explosions, and overly smooth AI surfaces.
- Young and premium: clean, tactile, slightly fashionable, but not luxury-advertising loud.
- Unified taste, varied scenes: do not repeat the same window-sill/tabletop layout across the set.

## Scene Rotation

Each category must rotate through multiple scene families so the cards feel curated rather than templated.

Weather scene families:

- Indoor light: bedding, wall, curtain, cup, mirror, floor.
- City surface: wet asphalt, stairs, subway entrance, convenience-store awning, crosswalk, railing.
- Nature detail: leaf, grass, stone, water, snow, branch.
- Carried object: umbrella, coat, scarf, tote, headphones, book edge.
- Sky fragment: cloud edge, sunset plane, storm darkening, pale blue opening.

Scent scene families:

- Raw material: tea, wood, petals, citrus peel, resin, salt, leaves.
- Personal space: bathroom shelf, closet, desk, entry table, cafe counter.
- Tactile surface: cotton, paper, ceramic, metal tray, stone, lacquer, leather.
- Natural trace: wet stone, flower shadow, bark, sea salt, rain leaf.
- Ritual detail: incense dish, tea glass, folded note, small bottle, candle not lit.

## Weather Set

Weather images describe external atmosphere and inner state. Prefer surfaces, sky fragments, light, fabric, carried objects, city traces, and water marks over literal weather icons.

- `first-snow`: clean concrete stair edge, thin fresh snow, black wool glove, quiet cold light.
- `morning-fog`: city railing fading into pale grey fog, one blurred bicycle shape, matte silver tones.
- `partly-cloudy`: white sheet on bed with a sharp blue-sky light patch and soft cloud shadow.
- `typhoon-eve`: empty convenience-store awning at night, wind-bent transparent umbrella, tense green-grey light.
- `plum-rain`: wet stone path, folded dark umbrella edge, long humid grey, muted moss accent.
- `sunset-glow`: warm orange light on a plain wall, glass cup rim, long elegant shadow.
- `after-rain`: black asphalt puddle reflecting a traffic light, one green leaf, clean air.
- `dry-wind`: beige linen coat on chair, open book edge lifting slightly, pale hard light.
- `humid-night`: bathroom mirror corner with condensation, small amber lamp reflection, soft dark tile.
- `clear-cold`: brushed metal tray outdoors, crisp blue sky reflection, sharp winter shadow.
- `spring-shower`: small raindrops on fresh branch over white ceramic tile, light green accent.
- `summer-heat`: iced glass on concrete ledge, strong sun stripe, saturated cobalt shadow.
- `autumn-cloud`: muted sky above a quiet pedestrian bridge, beige coat fabric entering frame.
- `winter-sun`: low winter sun across cream blanket and wooden floor, warm white quietness.
- `distant-thunder`: dark cloud slice over a rooftop railing, ceramic cup on concrete, charged stillness.

## Scent Set

Scent images are not product ads. They should suggest smell through materials and atmosphere: tea, flowers, stone, citrus peel, incense smoke, woods, cloth, paper.

- `cold-tea`: pale tea in glass cup, cool table, faint amber.
- `white-flower`: one white bloom, ceramic, morning shadow.
- `sandalwood`: wood block, cream paper, warm brown.
- `rain-stone`: wet stone surface, mineral grey, soft reflection.
- `citrus-peel`: citrus peel curl, white plate, green-yellow accent.
- `incense`: thin smoke line, dark dish, calm background.
- `fig-leaf`: green leaf, milky glass, sunlit edge.
- `iris-powder`: folded paper, pale violet, powdery softness.
- `sea-salt`: rough salt crystals, blue-grey cloth, clean air.
- `black-tea`: dark tea, lacquer tray, red-brown highlight.
- `cedar-closet`: cedar wood, folded cotton, quiet shadow.
- `rose-pepper`: muted rose petal, peppercorns, ivory plate.
- `mint-rain`: mint leaves, cool glass, wet tabletop.
- `amber-skin`: amber resin, warm linen, low golden light.
- `paper-musk`: old paper, clean cotton, soft beige grey.
